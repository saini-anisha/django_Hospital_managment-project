Ayushman Card Facilities

Introduction

The Ayushman Bharat scheme, also known as Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), aims to provide healthcare facilities to the underprivileged. The scheme offers financial protection for healthcare services.

Eligibility

  • Families identified by the SECC (Socio-Economic Caste Census) 2011 database.
  • Rural and urban poor families.
  • Specific occupational categories of urban workers.
  • Families with no adult male member aged 16 to 59 years.

Benefits

  • Coverage of up to â‚¹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.
  • Cashless and paperless access to services.
  • Includes pre-hospitalization and post-hospitalization expenses.
  • Comprehensive coverage including 3 days of pre-hospitalization and 15 days of post-hospitalization expenses.
  • All pre-existing conditions are covered from day one.

How to Apply

To apply for the Ayushman Bharat card, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official Ayushman Bharat website or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).
  2. Check eligibility by entering your mobile number and verifying with OTP.
  3. If eligible, proceed with the registration process by providing necessary documents like ID proof and address proof.
  4. Once verified, you will receive the Ayushman Bharat card.
